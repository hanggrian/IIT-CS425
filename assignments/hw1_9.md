> You are given the below functional dependencies for the
  relation $R(A\ B\ C\ D\ E\ F)$,
>
> $$
> \begin{array}{llll}
>   F = \{ \\
>   & A & \rightarrow & BC, \\
>   & C & \rightarrow & DA, \\
>   & D & \rightarrow & E, \\
>   & AD & \rightarrow & F \\
>   \}
> \end{array}
> $$

## Problem 1

> What are the keys for the relation?

### Step 1

$$
\begin{array}{lll}
  A & \rightarrow & B \\
  A & \rightarrow & C \\
  C & \rightarrow & D \\
  C & \rightarrow & A \\
  D & \rightarrow & E \\
  AD & \rightarrow & F
\end{array}
$$

### Step 2

$$
\begin{array}{lll}
  A? & D^+ & = & \{D\} \\
  D? & A^+ & = & \{A\} \\
  D? & F^+ & = & \{F\} \\
  F? & D^+ & = & \{D\} \\
\end{array}
$$
